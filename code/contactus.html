Refunds typically take 5-10 business days to process, depending on your payment method. Credit card refunds may appear on your next billing statement. For any refund inquiries, please contact our support team with your booking reference number.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and select cryptocurrency options. Some events may have specific payment restrictions.

For canceled events, we will automatically process refunds to the original payment method. For postponed events, your tickets will remain valid for the new date, or you may request a refund within 30 days of the postponement announcement.
