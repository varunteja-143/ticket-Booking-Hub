How to Book Bus Tickets

Booking your bus tickets is quick and easy with our platform. Follow these simple steps:

Select your departure and destination cities from the dropdown menus
Choose your travel date (or use quick options for today/tomorrow)
Click "Search Bus" to view available buses and schedules
Select your preferred bus, seat, and boarding point
Enter passenger details and make payment
Receive e-ticket via SMS and email

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a bus ticket online? +
You can book bus tickets online by selecting your route, travel date, choosing a bus, selecting seats, and making payment. The process takes just a few minutes!
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI payments, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay.
Can I cancel my bus ticket? +
Yes, you can cancel tickets online. Cancellation charges depend on how close to departure time you cancel. Check the cancellation policy for your specific booking.
How do I get my ticket after booking? +
You'll receive an e-ticket via SMS and email immediately after booking. You can also download it from your account on our website or app.
Is it safe to travel by bus during COVID-19? +
All buses on our platform follow strict safety protocols including regular sanitization, mandatory masks, and temperature checks. We recommend checking specific operator policies.
What should I do if I miss my bus? +
Contact our customer support immediately. Depending on the operator's policy, you may be able to board at the next stop or get partial credit for a future booking.
